ABOUT

I began my college career pursuing a degree in Graphic Design, mostly working with branding and print design. My lifelong passion has been to develop for the web, but the program I was in didn't feel like the right path for my desires, so I took a leap of faith and transitioned into a Computer Science degree program. I still love the design process that I learned in art school, but I also love being able to dig into the code and create more exciting and interactive experiences for users.

I graduated with a Bachelors in Computer Science from Texas A&M - Commerce, and I am currently pursuing my Masters in Computer Science at UT - Dallas, with a focus on Human and Computer Interaction. I am interested in developing user interfaces, websites, and other technology that can be used to interact with the digital world. I have an equal interest in both art and computer science, and aim to merge these two interests into beautiful user experiences.

As I move forward with Computer Science I hope to incorporate my artistic side more and learn to leverage the tools available to me. I continue to learn and grow as a developer every day. I have always had a passion for knowledge and plan to continue expanding my toolset as my career progresses.
